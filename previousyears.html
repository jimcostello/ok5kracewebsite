New View of Top Finishers since 2000

Ever wonder how finishing times stack up across the many years of the OK 5k? Well now you can see the Winning Male and Female runners for each year, the Top 10 Finishers Overall and the Top 10 Finishers in each 5-year age category for both Females and Males. Just click the bulleted links below.

  • OK5k Winners, Male and Female, 1999-2013
  • Top 10 Female Finishers Overall and Age Groups 2000-2012
  • Top 10 Male Finishers Overall and Age Groups 2000-2012
  • All Female Finishers 2000-2012
  • All Male Finishers 2000-2012

1999 Results (the first year of the race) are not included because the course was different and our timing clock broke. But it was still the start of something great!

OK 5k Tops 600 Registrants, 594 Finishers in 2013
The 2013 overall winners were:
  • 5K Men: Kevin Treadway , 15:15
  • 5K Women:Emily Bryans, 17:45
  • 1M Girl: Savanna Charrow, 6:32
  • 1M Boy: Robbie Wiltsie, 6:53
 The 2013 5K and 1-mile results are posted in the links below and on the Albany Running Exchange website (who did the chip timing and scoring for this year's race), with many different searches, sorts, and groupings. Results for the previous years' 5K and 1-mile Kid's race (overall and awards) can also be seen in the links below.
