Other Group Runs in Kinderhook

  • Saturday mornings at 7:00 AM in the Village Square. Meet by the Bandstand area. Various distances; various runners.
  • From September through June, every Wednesday night at 5:30 PM starting from the corner of Broad Street and Albany Avenue in the Village Square. Anywhere from two to ten runners show up to run 3-6 miles, rain, snow, sleet, or shine.
For interesting running routes, see the USATF Running Routes site (http://www.usatf.org/routes/search/).  Enter  "Kinderhook" in the CITY search box  to find many routes in the Kinderhook area. You can also map out your own routes and check saved routes in other locations.

Questions? Visit the Kinderhook Runners Club Website www.kinderhookrunnersclub.com
